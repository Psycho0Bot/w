# 📖 Overview

WealthOS is an AI-powered portfolio operating system designed to help retail investors manage, analyze, and optimize their investments using institutional-grade analytics and AI.

Instead of switching between multiple portfolio trackers, news websites, spreadsheets, and AI tools, WealthOS brings everything into one intelligent platform.

The platform combines portfolio management, market intelligence, financial news, AI decision support, analytics, and risk management into a seamless experience.

---

# ❗ Problem

Retail investors typically use:

- Excel
- Portfolio trackers
- Brokerage apps
- News websites
- AI chatbots
- Mutual fund apps
- Crypto trackers

None of these tools provide a unified investment intelligence platform.
